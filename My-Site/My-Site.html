The Classic All-American Burger

Many years ago, my sister and I, on our many adventures in exploration, stumbled into an old style diner in Bowling Green, KY. Chrome stools at the counter and only two or three tables. This was the real deal and long before such places became "retro" or a novelty. Later, we found ourselves in another such diner in Florida. The place still had the old signs up for "Colored" and "Whites" bathrooms, entrances, and water fountain. I remember knowing what it was but not understanding why. Regardless, both places made unbelievable hamburgers. Crisp leaf lettuce, fresh tomatoes, crunchy onions, pickles (which I picked off, thank you very much), loaded with condiments and then there was the burger! The most delicious, juiciest, hamburger topped with the perfect slice of cheese you have ever had. I have yet to have had a burger like that since then. But I keep trying and I keep perfecting my own burgers!
